About Us

Discover our story before you decide

  • about the company

Founded in 1985, Vasu Enterprises has been a trusted name in the world of grinders and kitchen machinery for nearly four decades. From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a leading supplier, serving both dealers and sellers with reliable, high-quality products that blend durability, efficiency, and innovation.

Our journey is built on a strong foundation of trust, customer satisfaction, and service excellence. Over the years, we have continuously evolved to meet the dynamic needs of the market, while staying true to our core values of quality and commitment.

At Vasu Enterprises, we don’t just deliver machines — we deliver partnerships. Whether you are a retailer, distributor, or an end-user, our goal is to provide the right solutions that power your kitchen and your business.

1985

Founded Vasu Enterprises with a vision to provide high-quality grinders and kitchen machinery.

1990s

Expanded sales and after-sales service network, becoming a trusted choice for dealers and sellers.

2000s

Set up our own manufacturing unit, ensuring better quality control and innovation.

2010s

Achieved Pan-India delivery, catering to customers across every state with reliable logistics.

2020s

Entered the global market, proudly exporting our products to international clients.
